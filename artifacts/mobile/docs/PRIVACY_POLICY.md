# Privacy Policy: GMS Complaints Box 🔒

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the GMS Complaints Box application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Data**: Name, email, and role (Founder, Supervisor, Client).
- **Service Data**: Sites, complaints, and operational metrics reported through the app.
- **Log Data**: System events and activity logs for audit purposes.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the facility management service.
- To facilitate real-time communication between Founders, Supervisors, and Clients.
- To generate business analytics and operational reports.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We use **Supabase** (powered by AWS/PostgreSQL) for secure data storage. Data is protected using Row Level Security (RLS) and industry-standard encryption.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party tools (like Expo) for app distribution and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies.

## 5. Your Rights
You can access and update your profile information directly through the application settings.

## 6. Contact Us
For privacy concerns, please contact your organization's GMS Administrator.
